Yes, we integrate seamlessly with popular CRM platforms like Salesforce, HubSpot, Zoho, and local Malaysian systems. Custom integrations are available for enterprise clients with existing tools.

How quickly can we see improvements in customer satisfaction?

Most businesses see immediate improvements in response times (within 24 hours of deployment). Customer satisfaction scores typically increase by 30-40% within the first month as customers appreciate faster, more accurate responses.
